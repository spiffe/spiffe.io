Community Day 2026

Celebrating ten years of SPIFFE.

In 2026, SPIFFE turns ten. To mark a decade of open, vendor-neutral workload identity, we're planning a SPIFFE Community Day - a chance to bring the community together, celebrate how far the project has come, and help shape where it goes next.

Past community days have typically blended a few things: talks from teams running SPIFFE and SPIRE in production, sharing real success stories along with the implementation details and lessons behind them; updates on the ongoing specification work that keeps the standard moving forward; and time for the more novel, exploratory topics that point to where workload identity is heading. Expect a similar mix this time around, with plenty of room to learn from - and talk to - the people building and using these projects.

Nothing's set in stone yet - not the date, the location, or the programme - and that's exactly where you come in. The single most useful thing you can do today is tell us you're interested.
